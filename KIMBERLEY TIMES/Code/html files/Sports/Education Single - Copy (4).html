Hugo Broos aims for a win against Zim

Molwane,Kamogelo

2021 October 21

Hugo Broos, the coach of Bafana Bafana, is aiming for a win against Zimbabwe in their next 2022 Qatar FIFA World Cup qualifier next month.

"But first and foremost, we have to deal with Zimbabwe, and I'm hoping that my colleague (Ethiopia coach Wubetu Abate) will give us a little gift next month by beating Ghana to make things easier for us." Bafana Bafana played in front of approximately 2,000 fans, and Broos stated that he would like to have more against Zimbabwe.

"The danger was constant, and it was extremely difficult for us to keep the ball." We suffered a little bit, but on the other hand, we were able to score on the set-piece, which was something we knew would be possible for us." The most important thing was to win and remain first in our group after four games, which is exactly what we aimed for. We had a terrible trip to Ethiopia last year.
